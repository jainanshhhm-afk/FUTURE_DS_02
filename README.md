# FUTURE_DS_02
Customer Churn & Retention Analysis 📊

📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of Task 2 for the Future Interns Data Analytics Internship. The primary objective was to analyze customer data for a subscription-based business to identify churn patterns, key retention drivers, and actionable trends to reduce customer loss.

🛠️ Tools & Skills
Tools: Power BI, Excel.

Skills Gained: Retention analysis, cohort analysis, customer lifetime metrics, and insight-driven decision-making.

🔍 Key Insights
Based on the dashboard analysis, the following patterns were identified:

High-Risk Period: The highest churn occurs within the first 6 months of customer tenure.

Contract Influence: Customers on Month-to-month contracts show significantly higher churn rates compared to long-term contracts.

Service Friction: Fiber optic users and customers using electronic check payments are at a higher risk of churning.

Price Correlation: Higher monthly charges are directly correlated with increased churn risk.

💡 Business Recommendations
Contract Incentives: Promote long-term contracts with specific incentives to move users away from month-to-month plans.

Onboarding Engagement: Improve the early customer onboarding experience to stabilize retention in the first 6 months.

Payment Optimization: Encourage the adoption of automated payment methods over electronic checks.

Targeted Retention: Offer personalized retention packages to high-risk customers, specifically those in high-billing tiers or using Fiber optic services.
